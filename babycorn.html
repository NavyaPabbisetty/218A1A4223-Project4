Babycorn

Recipe:

Baby corn (also known as young corn, cornlettes or baby sweetcorn) is a
cereal grain taken from corn (maize) harvested early while the stalks
are still small and immature. It typically is eaten whole—including
the cob, which is otherwise too tough for human consumption in
mature corn—in raw, pickled, and cooked forms. Baby corn is common
in stir fry dishes.
