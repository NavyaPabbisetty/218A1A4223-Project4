Panner Tikka

Recipe:

Paneer tikka added as a salad in the diet can be used
to increase the protein intake as well as using it in
the process of a weight-loss program. The benefit of
paneer with the marinated mixture provides the perfect
combination of a healthy diet with taste.Paneer Tikka
is a North Indian appetizer where chunks of paneer are
marinated in spiced yogurt and grilled in a Tandoor,
a traditional clay oven. The term Paneer refers
to Indian cottage cheese and Tikka translates to
chunks of grilled meat, or a vegetarian option, in
this case it is the paneer.
