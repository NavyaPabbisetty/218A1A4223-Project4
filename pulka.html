Pulka

Recipe:

Indian diet is incomplete without phulka or chapati.
While they both are made of wheat flour, they both
are different.Chapati is a type of roti, and often
the words are used interchangeably. In my lexicon,
chapati is made on a tawa only and roasted with oil
or ghee. Whereas phulka are partially cooked on the
tawa and later puffed on direct flame. Also Chapati
is usually larger in size than a phulka.
